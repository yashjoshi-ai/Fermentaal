The founder's story

It started with a single jar.

FermenTaal began on a kitchen counter in Dehradun, with one glass jar, a stubborn SCOBY, and the belief that a drink could be alive.

We were tired of sodas that were all sugar and no soul — so we brewed our own. Every bottle is still made by hand, in small batches, with real pineapple and nothing artificial. No factory. No shortcuts. Just patience, wild ferment, and clean Himalayan air.

This is kombucha the way it's meant to be: honest, fizzy, and proudly homemade.
